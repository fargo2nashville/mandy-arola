Meet Mandy Arola

Mandy Arola is Nashville Software School’s chief content wrangler, a.k.a. marketing manager. She is responsible for all forms of marketing content, including blogs, social media, the school's website, e-mails, event banners, flyers, and more. Additionally, she designs most of this content.

She is active in Nashville's tech community and regularly attends meetups. This summer she is helping promote ProductCamp Nashville as their blog writer.

Mandy is an active member of the American Marketing Association's (AMA) Nashville chapter and currently serves as the Chair of the Entertainment & Sports Special Interest Group.

She spoke at Barcamp Nashville in 2017 and Craft Content Nashville in 2018. She is currently seeking new speaking opportunities.

She believes in generosity, integrity, and hard work. Mandy has a passion for innovation, marketing, and the user experience. She loves storytelling and understanding the consumer’s wants and needs. Her strong analytical skills balanced with a love for creativity makes her an asset to any organization. Most importantly, Mandy is always willing to learn and add to her skillset.

When she's not working or giving her time to local professional organizations, you can find her photographing the world or at home with her beloved dog Lola.
